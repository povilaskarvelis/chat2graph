About Chat2Graph

Chat2Graph is a clinical knowledge graph analysis tool that transforms mental health interview transcripts into structured, queryable graph data. Using advanced NLP and LLM technology, we extract clinical entities (symptoms, treatments, triggers) and semantic entities (people, places, concepts) to build comprehensive knowledge graphs.

Our goal is to provide clinicians and researchers with powerful visualization and analysis tools for understanding patterns in clinical conversations across different mental health disorders.
